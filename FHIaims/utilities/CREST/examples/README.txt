
An example for the use of CREST+AIMS for the "clean-cleaved" surface of Si(111),
with n-type doping concentration of 1e17 1/cm^3.

Ofer Sinai, 2015
For more info, see:
O. Sinai, O. T. Hofmann, P. Rinke, M. Scheffler. G. Heimel and L. Kronik,
Phys. Rev. B (2015)

Example files:
=================================
crest input file: crest.in
crest output file: crest.out (reflects more-or-less what you should get)
